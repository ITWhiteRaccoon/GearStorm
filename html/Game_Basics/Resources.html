Before you can build or craft anything in GearStorm you will need resources. Resources consist of minerals you find by mining in the ground, materials you gather from plants and rocks, and things you find around the world. Minerals: Most minerals you find buried by mining in the world, you will see minerals on the surface marked with their periodic element symbols (ie: "Cu" for Copper) on your mini map and main map when you get close enough.
